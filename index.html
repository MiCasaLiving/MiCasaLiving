Our Story

A family business
built on two roots

We are a husband and wife team — originally from México, now based between San Diego and our properties south of the border. For five years we have been quietly building something we believe in: safe, beautiful, well-managed homes for Americans who choose México as their home.

We know both worlds. We understand what an American renter needs — reliability, clear communication, and knowing someone will actually pick up the phone. And we know México — the neighborhoods, the buildings, the people, the culture.

Every apartment in our portfolio has been personally selected. We wouldn't rent it to you if we wouldn't live there ourselves. Mi Casa Living is our legacy — and we intend to build it one happy tenant at a time.
